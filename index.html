Bio

The craft, the storytelling, the way a song can hit you square in the chest — that’s the territory.

A Marin County solo act on acoustic guitar. Covers and originals, chosen because there’s something real in them worth hearing again — something that comes straight from the heart and lands with intention.

For fans of Springsteen · Zach Bryan · Mumford and Sons · Radiohead · Bob Dylan · Coldplay · Glen Hansard · Death Cab for Cutie · Sara Bareilles · Tracy Chapman
